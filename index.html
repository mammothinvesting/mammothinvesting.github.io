Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate are the stock forecasts?
Our stock forecasts are based on historical data and advanced AI algorithms, but they are not guarantees of future performance. Market conditions can change rapidly, and actual results may vary.
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password?" link on the Sign in/Sign up screen. Follow the instructions to reset your password using the email address associated with your account.
Is my personal data secure?
The Company will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that Your data is treated securely and in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Cancel a subscription
  1. Go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions.
  2. Tap a subscription, then tap Cancel Subscription or Cancel All Services.
How do I contact support?
You can contact our support team by emailing us at sahilsingh@mammothinvesting.com. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Troubleshooting

App Crashing or Issues Getting the Forecast or Analysis

  • Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed.
  • Try restarting your device.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • If the issue persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs.

Login Issues

  • Make sure you are using the correct email address and password.
  • If you forgot your password, reset it using the "Forgot Password" link.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • If you are still unable to log in, contact support.

Data Not Updating

  • Close the app completely and open it again.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Try refreshing the data within the app.
  • If the issue persists, contact support at sahilsingh@mammothinvesting.com.
